# quotes that hit me hard

Some ideas stay with you long after you've read them.

These aren't just quotes I saved because they sounded good. They're reminders I've come back to whenever I needed a different perspective, a push to keep going, or a reason to start.

## A Few That Changed My Thinking

> **"You become what you think about most."**  
> — Earl Nightingale

> **"The best investment you can make is in yourself."**  
> — Warren Buffett

> **"Success is the best revenge."**  
> — Frank Sinatra *(commonly attributed)*

> **"The key to success is freedom, and the key to freedom is money."**  
> — Naval Ravikant

> **"Sometimes all you need is twenty seconds of insane courage."**  
> — Benjamin Mee

> **"The only way to predict the future is to create it."**  
> — Peter Drucker

> **"Discipline equals freedom."**  
> — Jocko Willink

> **"You miss 100 percent of the shots you don't take."**  
> — Wayne Gretzky

> **"If you're going through hell, keep going."**  
> — Winston Churchill

> **"The obstacle is the way."**  
> — Marcus Aurelius

> **"The harder you work, the luckier you get."**  
> — Gary Player

> **"Opportunities don't happen, you create them."**  
> — Chris Grosser

## Why I Keep Coming Back to Them

Every quote reminds me of something different.

Some push me to take action.

Some remind me to stay disciplined.

Others remind me that the future isn't something you wait for—it's something you build.

Whenever I feel stuck or uncertain, I read through this list again.

One sentence is usually enough to change my mindset for the rest of the day.

---

Maybe these words will stay with you the same way they've stayed with me.
